**Privacy Policy for CrowdLink**

*Last updated: March 2026*

CrowdLink is an offline peer-to-peer application designed to work without internet connectivity, mobile networks, or any external infrastructure. This privacy policy explains how the application handles user data.

**Data Collection**

CrowdLink does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties. There are no servers, no backend infrastructure, and no analytics or tracking of any kind. The developers have no access to any information about users or their usage of the application.

**Data Stored on Device**

The following information is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it except as described under Communication below:

- Your chosen display name, entered during onboarding
- Device identifiers used to identify your device to paired friends
- Messages exchanged with paired friends
- GPS coordinates when location sharing is enabled
- A list of paired friends and their associated identifiers

All of this data is stored in a local database on your device. Uninstalling the application removes all stored data permanently.

**Communication**

CrowdLink communicates directly between paired devices using Bluetooth Low Energy. No data is routed through the internet or any external server at any point. Messages and location data are transmitted only to devices that you have explicitly paired with by scanning their QR code. Intermediate devices may forward encrypted payloads as part of the mesh relay system but cannot read the content of those payloads.

**Permissions**

CrowdLink requests the following device permissions:

- Bluetooth — required for device discovery and messaging
- Location — required by Android for Bluetooth Low Energy scanning
- Camera — required for QR code scanning during friend pairing
- Notifications — required to alert you to incoming messages
- Vibration — required for SOS alert functionality

Location permission is used solely to enable Bluetooth scanning as required by Android. Your GPS coordinates are only shared with paired friends when location sharing is explicitly enabled in Settings.

**Children**

CrowdLink is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

**Changes to This Policy**

Any future changes to this policy will be reflected in an updated version posted with the application listing.
